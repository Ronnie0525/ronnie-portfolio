My Story

Experiences from Dubai

I'm Ronnie Balonon Jr., a multi-disciplinary designer based in Dubai, UAE. My journey into design started with a deep curiosity for how visuals communicate ideas — and it hasn't stopped since.

From UI/UX and web design to photography, video editing, graphic design, and AI-powered workflows, I bring a versatile creative toolkit to every project. I believe great design isn't just about looking good — it's about solving real problems and creating meaningful connections.

Whether I'm wireframing a mobile app, shooting a product campaign, or laying out print collateral, my goal is always the same: deliver work that's clean, functional, and visually powerful.
