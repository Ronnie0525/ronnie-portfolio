Video Editing

Cutting cinematic stories from raw footage.

Polished brand reels, short-form content, and cinematic cuts with colour-graded finishes.

What I Edit

Every frame considered — pacing, sound, and color tuned for impact across short-form and long-form.

Short-Form & Reels

Punchy 15–60s vertical cuts for Reels, TikTok, and Shorts — hook-led, captioned, and built for the algorithm.

Brand Films & Promos

Story-driven brand films and product promos — narrative pacing, B-roll, and a cinematic feel for hero campaigns.

Color Grading

Lumetri and DaVinci grades — consistent looks across clips, skin-tone correction, and cinematic LUTs.

Motion Graphics & Titles

Animated lower-thirds, kinetic type, logo stings, and lightweight VFX to lift brand identity across every cut.

Sound Design & Mix

Music selection, SFX layering, ducking, and clean dialogue — broadcast-ready audio that lands every emotional beat.

Cinematic Cut & Polish

J-cuts, L-cuts, match cuts, and rhythm work — tightening every edit so it flows like a story, not a slideshow.
